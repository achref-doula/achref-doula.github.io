Hi, I'm Achref. 👋

I am a Post-Doctoral Researcher at the Telecooperation Lab, Technical University of Darmstadt, working under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Max Mühlhäuser. I obtained my PhD (Summa Cum Laude) at TU Darmstadt in 2025 (my dissertation). Previously, I was a Visiting Post-Doc at Tongji University in Shanghai.
My research focuses on building safe, transparent, and reliable AI systems for dynamic urban environments. I work on uncertainty quantification (especially conformal prediction), neuro-symbolic reasoning, LLM-based planning and perception, and human-AI interaction for autonomous systems. I am particularly interested in providing formal safety guarantees for AI models operating in safety-critical settings.
During my PhD, I led the federally funded SemUrban project (€100K, Software Campus / BMBF) on semantically enriched deep learning for autonomous driving. I have published 10+ peer-reviewed papers at top venues including CoRL, IROS, CVPR, ISMAR, NAACL, and ICRA, with 40+ citations.
